About

The BPC Art Studio has sent hundreds of high school students to the top art schools and various comprehensive universities in the United States for many years. As long as students can strictly follow our plan, students can go from zero basis of painting to entering the top art academy. The art studio's curriculum will be taught by Baoping Chen, a professor of art at the San Francisco Art University and will be aided by a team of teachers with professional education experience.

Courses:

Drawing Courses: Elementary, Intermediate, Advanced, and University-level

Design Courses: Graphic design, Color composition, Digital painting, and other basic knowledge

Painting Courses: Elementary, Intermediate, and Advanced-level

Printmaking / Mixed Materials: Learning the basic skills of printmaking and using integrated materials.

Creation: Learn to find the unique artistic expression language, using different materials, performance, and techniques, showing the feelings of life and unique ideas.

Art Appreciation: Visit art museums and art appreciation lectures to enhance the level of artistic appreciation.

Classes

Regular classes include all ages. The advanced classes are divided into high and low age groups. On the basis of systematic teaching, the school teaches in the form of individual counseling according to the characteristics of each student. In particular, high school students who are preparing to apply for art colleges will receive individual professional counseling on their art portfolio. Classes on time, late leave early leave, no remedial classes. In special cases, you can discuss the time of remediation with the teacher.
